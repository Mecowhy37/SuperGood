THE SUPERGOOD FAMILY

When it comes to nutrition, we were craving thoughtfully-sourced products made with radical kindness. SUPERGOOD is our way of taking a stand — we created the convenience food we longed to buy and pakaged it conveniently and repsonsibly.

our local farmers

What we create starts at the soil. We partner with local farmers to source our ingredients ogranic and local-when-possible to be familiar with the growing practices and ensure we are getting quality produce grown in a sustainable manner. We also help local farmers reduce food waste to bring you farm-to-frozen goodness.

our chefs

Our first product prototypes were created together with a Nutritionist to ensure the combinations are totally wholesome and nutrient dense.

With a Michelin Star Chef onboard the products have the best of what Danish local produce can offer and of course there’s the taste!

our sustainable design experts

Working with recyclable packaging experts and one of Copenhagen’s top branding agencies to bring you nutritious options in a fun and inspiring way.

our team

We're a small yet dedicated group with the common goal of inspiring one another and others to live a healthier lifestyle, full of superfoods and balanced nutrition.

We're also very fortunate to have the many students and interns that jumped in to learn and contribute to our brand that aligns with the world they want to live in.

and you of course!
